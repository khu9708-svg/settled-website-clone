How to open locally (Windows)
  1. Keep this file in content/launch-videos/ next to the exports-v2/ folder.
  2. Double-click watch-local.html, or run open-player.bat in this folder.
  3. Or right-click the HTML file → Open with → your browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox).
  4. Click a title below to switch videos. Use the player controls for play, pause, volume, and fullscreen.
