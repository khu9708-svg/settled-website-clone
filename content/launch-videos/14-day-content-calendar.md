# 14-Day Content Calendar

## Posting cadence
- Daily: 1 primary short (TikTok + Shorts)
- Optional: 1 story/repost reminder
- Weekly: 1 pinned educational recap

## Platform adaptation notes
- TikTok: Faster hook in first 1.2s, heavier text overlays, more direct comment CTA.
- Shorts: Cleaner framing, slightly slower intro pacing, stronger SEO title + description, end-screen CTA.

## Repurposing matrix
- One master 35–45s source ->
  - TikTok v1: 22–32s hard cut
  - Shorts v1: 28–40s cleaner pacing
  - Story cut: 12–15s hook-only
  - Comment reply cut: 15–20s with one Q/A overlay

## Weekly optimization checklist
1. Keep top-retention first 2 seconds and reuse variants.
2. Replace underperforming hook line before rewriting full script.
3. Promote highest-save video again 5–7 days later with new caption.
4. Keep compliance line in caption or end card each post.
5. Track: 3s hold, avg watch, saves, profile clicks, site clicks.
